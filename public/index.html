About

Our Mission

The Crime Investigation Department, Ghana is an organization which deals with investigating and preventing crimes in Ghana. Our job is to provide information to private and public security organisations about crime to help them make informed decisions and ultimately curb the rise of crimes in Ghana.

What we do

We are the Data Analytics Team Of the Criminal Investigation Department, Ghana. We specialize in analyzing, visualizing and communicating data generated by the Department in order to make meaningful decisions based on this data about what crimes have taken place in the past and how best to improve security and prevent the loss of lives in the future. We work tirelessly to ensure that our data is correct, simple yet meaningful to the Department and the general public at large.
